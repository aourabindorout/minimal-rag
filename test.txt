XYZ TEXTILES LIMITED
Employee Work and Leave Policy
Version 3.2

---

## WORKING HOURS

The standard working hours at XYZ Textiles Limited
are from 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday through Friday.

Employees are expected to be present in the office
for at least 8 hours per working day.

A grace period of 15 minutes is allowed for late
arrival. More than 3 late arrivals in a month
result in one half-day leave deduction.

---

## REMOTE WORK POLICY

Employees may work remotely for up to 6 days per
calendar month.

Remote work requests must be submitted at least
24 hours in advance and approved by the reporting
manager.

Remote work is not permitted during quarterly
inventory audits.

---

## CASUAL LEAVE

Each employee receives 12 casual leave days per
calendar year.

Unused casual leave expires at the end of the
calendar year and cannot be carried forward.

---

## SICK LEAVE

Each employee receives 8 sick leave days per year.

For absences exceeding 2 consecutive days,
a medical certificate must be submitted.

---

## ANNUAL LEAVE

Employees receive 20 annual leave days per year.

Up to 10 unused annual leave days may be carried
forward to the next calendar year.

---

## SPECIAL LEAVE

Employees are granted:

* 5 days marriage leave
* 10 days paternity leave
* 90 days maternity leave

---

## TRAVEL POLICY

Employees traveling for company business receive:

* Economy class air travel for domestic trips
* Reimbursement up to ₹2,500 per night for hotel stay
* Daily meal allowance of ₹800

---

## CONFIDENTIALITY

Employees must not share company financial data,
supplier contracts, or customer information with
external parties without written authorization.

Violation of confidentiality policy may result in
termination of employment.

---

## IT POLICY

Employees must change their system password every
60 days.

Personal USB devices are not permitted on company
machines without approval from the IT department.
